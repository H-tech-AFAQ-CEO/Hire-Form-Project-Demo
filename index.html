Background Check Process
Required

Purpose: Initiate background check process for employment.

Toledo Ark requires all new hires to complete a BCI/FBI background check.

Background Check Instructions

Step 1: Go to Express Fingerprinting Service

Step 2: Use the following codes:

  • BCI Code: BCI-5164.34
  • FBI Code: FBI-4757.101

Step 3: Complete fingerprinting and pay required fees

Step 4: Results will be sent directly to Toledo Ark
